How it works
From numerous sites to
all resouces in one page

A RAG navigation engine maps your need to a specific building, then routes you there with campus shortcuts.

01
🗣️

Describe your need

Type anything in plain language: "I'm a trans student who needs to update my name." No specific keywords needed.

02
🔍

Intent is understood

We search UofT's PDFs and websites to match your worry to the right office, room, and hours.

03
🗺️

Fastest route found

Google Maps draws your path using a UofT shortcut graph, accessible routes included.

04
✨

Plain language summary

Amazon Bedrock compacts websites and PDFs into one sentence: "Bring your passport to the 2nd floor."
